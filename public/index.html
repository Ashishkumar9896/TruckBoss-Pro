Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new truck?

Go to the 'Trucks' tab and click the 'Add Truck' button in the top right corner.

How is profit calculated?

Profit = Trip Amount - Fuel Cost.

What is the Maintenance Forecast?

It predicts service needs based on the number of trips completed since the last maintenance record.

How do Notifications work?

Click the Bell Icon in the top right to open your notifications panel. You will receive real-time alerts for Trucks that are Overdue for Maintenance (Over 15 trips) and Trips that are marked as Pending.

How do I view Fuel Volume and Cost together?

In both the Dashboard and the Fuel Efficiency tab, the Fuel trend graphs are beautifully styled Bar Charts. Simply hover your mouse over any month's bar to see a sleek tooltip displaying both the Total Cost (₹) and the exact Volume (Liters) purchased.

Can I see which driver purchased the fuel when I export records?

Yes, go to the Fuel Records tab, apply any filters you want, and click Export Excel. The spreadsheet will automatically include the assigned Driver Name alongside the Truck details and Cost.
