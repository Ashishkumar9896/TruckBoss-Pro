Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new truck?

Go to the 'Trucks' tab and click the 'Add Truck' button in the top right corner.

How is profit calculated?

Profit = (Total Amount) - (Fuel Cost + Tolls + Misc Expenses).

What is the Maintenance Forecast?

It predicts service needs based on the number of trips completed since the last maintenance record.
